A Tribute to Philadelphia Sports

City of Brotherly Love

The history of Philadelphia all began in 1682, when the city was founded by William Penn.

Philadelphia is a sports city through and through. Philadelphia is one of twelve cities that hosts teams in all four major sports leagues in North America, and Philadelphia is one of just three cities in which one team from every league plays within city limits. The four major teams include the Philadelphia Phillies, Philadelphia Eagles, Philadelphia Flyers, and Philadelphia 76ers.
